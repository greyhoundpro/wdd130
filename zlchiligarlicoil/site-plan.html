How To Make Chili Garlic Oil For The Perfect Dinner
Chili garlic oil is a staple in many Chinese restaurants, but what if you want to make this delicious condiment at home? Follow this easy recipe and find out!
How to Make Chili Garlic Oil
If you're looking for a delicious way to add some flavor to your dinner, look no further than chili garlic oil. This easy-to-make oil is perfect for drizzling over pasta, rice, or vegetables. Here's how to make it:
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup chopped garlic
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a small saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes, and cook for about 1 minute, until the garlic is fragrant.
3. Remove from the heat and let cool slightly. Pour into a glass jar or bottle and store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
Recipes That Use Chili Garlic Oil
If you're looking for a delicious way to add some spice to your dinner, look no further than chili garlic oil. This easy-to-make condiment is the perfect addition to any meal, and can be used in a variety of recipes. Here are some of our favorite ways to use chili garlic oil:
-Toss it with roasted vegetables for a quick and easy side dish.
-Add it to homemade pasta sauce for an extra kick of flavor.
-Stir it into soups or stews for a touch of heat.
- Use it as a dipping sauce for breadsticks or other appetizers.
So what are you waiting for? Give chili garlic oil a try in your next meal!
