Our Story

ZL Chili Garlic Oil is owned and founded by Jayvee Galosmo and Marionne Mae Galosmo who are food lovers and who just wants to make any Ulam enjoyable.

The idea that started their journey towards making this delicacy is from making a Chili Garlic Oil to be partnered with siomai being sold to the community.
The customers who got a taste of the Chili Garlic Oil wanted the creators to sell in cans and so started the venture of creating the masterpiece.

About Us

ZL Foods is dedicated in providing fresh and quality Chili Garlic Oil products that will help you satisfy your cravings.
Our recipe was created with an idea of making any dish satisfying to the taste and we found it to be so good that we want to share it with the people we love.
